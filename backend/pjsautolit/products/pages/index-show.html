About Us

Vast Collection

PJ's Auto Literature has over 100,000 items in stock and ready to ship. If you do not see the item you are looking for on the website email us at pjsautolit@aol.com to inquire if we have it in stock, and if we don't have it, we have sources that may be able to fill your specific needs.

Trusted Source

PJ's Auto Literature has been buying, selling and trading automotive sales brochures since 1988. Our eBay store has 99.9% positive feedback with over 136K of total sales.

History

Between 1988 and the present time we have added owner's manuals, shop manuals, magazine ads, and color charts. Our inventory includes literature for: cars, trucks, motorcycles, tractors and other motorized vehicles. E-mail your specific requests and we will reply within 48 hours.
